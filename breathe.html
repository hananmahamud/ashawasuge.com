November 3, 2019

This video was created to motivate and encourage people to trust the process. I want the world to understand that the journey is going to be hard, but you can overcome any obstacles if you trust yourself. As a person who constantly feels out of place, I wanted to create a poetry video (with the best people) to remind others that we can craft a safe space for ourselves by uplifting one another. Be kind to yourself and know how strong you are.

you are loved.
you are seen.
and we are so proud of you!

B.R.E.A.T.H.E: Close your eyes. take a second and remember how far you have come.
Celebrate the small accomplishments and keep pushing.

I love y’all. I support y’all. And I am on a mission to creating a table for y’all to sit at without any judgment or fear of not fitting in. In this space- you are free to be who you are and be whatever your heart desires.

Sisterly love,
A.W
